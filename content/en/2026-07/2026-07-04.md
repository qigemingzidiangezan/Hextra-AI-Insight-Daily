## **AI Content Summary**
Today's AI landscape is quite dynamic, with major players making significant moves and facing challenges. OpenAI has proposed donating 5% of its equity to a U.S. sovereign wealth fund, aiming to share AI profits and ease regulatory pressure. Meanwhile, Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg admitted that AI Agent progress hasn't met expectations, alongside a broader market reflection on the over-hyping of AI. Despite this, major tech giants continue to invest heavily in AI infrastructure and applications: Meta launched an experimental AI gaming app, Anthropic is exploring custom chip development with Samsung and eyeing drug discovery, and Microsoft established a new $2.5 billion AI deployment company.
