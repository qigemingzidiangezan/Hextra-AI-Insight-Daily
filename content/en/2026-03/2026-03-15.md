### Cutting-Edge Research

1.  **Shanghai AI Lab proposes knowledge-reasoning decoupling framework DRIFT.**
    Shanghai AI Lab has proposed the DRIFT knowledge-reasoning decoupling framework. Small models handle "reading," while large models focus on "thinking." `[DRIFT Achieves Efficient Knowledge-Reasoning Decoupling (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10021)` has definitely caught the academic world's attention. With a 3B knowledge model combined with a 7B reasoning model, performance after **32x compression** comes close to, or even surpasses, full-text input. Reasoning latency stays super low across all context lengths. Even without specific safety training, this architecture significantly boosts robustness on multiple safety benchmarks. The research team also extended this idea to `[Protein Understanding Task (AI News)](https://github.com/Lancelot-Xie/DRIFT)` for further validation.
