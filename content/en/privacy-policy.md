### Tracking Tech & Cookies 🍪

**Cookies and similar tracking tech** are what we use to keep an eye on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking technologies we deploy include beacons, tags, and scripts, all designed to collect and track info, helping us to amp up and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file popped onto your Device. You can tell your browser to reject all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one is being sent. But, if you don't accept Cookies, you might find yourself locked out of certain parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to give Cookies the cold shoulder, our Service just might use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like noting the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

**Cookies** come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

**We use both Session and Persistent Cookies** for the following awesome purposes:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Session Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial to hook you up with services available through the Website and to let you flex its features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to get those services to you.

-   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are here to figure out if users have given the green light to the use of cookies on the Website.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preference. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you use the Website.

**Want more info on the Cookies we use and your options concerning them?** Check out our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
