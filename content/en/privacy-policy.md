### Tracking Tech & Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking technologies we're talking about include beacons, tags, and scripts. They help us gather and track info, and seriously level up our Service through analysis. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file that gets placed on your device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But heads up: if you don't accept Cookies, some parts of our Service might not play nice. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to give Cookies the boot, our Service might just use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these little electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These guys let the Company (for example) count users who've visited certain pages or opened an email, and they're used for other related website stats (like tracking how popular a section is and making sure our systems and servers are all good to go).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even after you've gone offline, while Session Cookies get wiped the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital for hookin' you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help verify users and put a stop to any sketchy use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't be delivered, and we only use 'em to give you those specific services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about figuring out if users have given a nod to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're on the Website, like keeping your login deets or language preferences in mind. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized vibe and save you the hassle of typing in your preferences every single time you use the Website.

For the full scoop on the Cookies we use and your options regarding them, just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
