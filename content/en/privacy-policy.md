### Tracking Technologies and Cookies 🍪
We're totally into using Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep an eye on activity within our Service and store certain bits of info. This tracking tech includes cool stuff like beacons, tags, and scripts – all used to gather and monitor info, helping us refine and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

*   **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file that gets plonked onto your device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or to ping you when one's trying to send. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might find some parts of our Service aren't playing ball. 🤷‍♀️ Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to give Cookies the boot, our Service might just use 'em.
*   **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain these dinky electronic files called Web Beacons (you might also hear them called clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs). These clever little guys allow the Company, for instance, to tally up users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other neat website stats (like checking out how popular a certain section is and making sure our systems and servers are all good).

Cookies? They can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies hang out on your personal computer or mobile device even after you've logged off, while Session Cookies totally disappear the second you shut down your web browser. Poof! 💨

We roll with both Session and Persistent Cookies for a few key reasons:

*   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    *   **Type**: Session Cookies
    *   **Administered by**: Yours truly (Us!)
    *   **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely, positively crucial for making sure you get the services available through the Website and can use its awesome features. They're like the bouncers that help verify who's who and keep out any fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't work, and we *only* use these specific Cookies to hook you up.

*   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
    *   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    *   **Administered by**: Us, again!
    *   **Purpose**: These Cookies are basically our little helpers to see if you've already given us the green light to use cookies on the Website. ✅

*   **Functionality Cookies**
    *   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    *   **Administered by**: Still us!
    *   **Purpose**: These Cookies are super handy because they help us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website. Think remembering your login deets or your preferred language. The whole idea here is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the annoyance of re-entering your preferences every single time you swing by the Website. Phew!

Wanna dive deeper into the Cookies we use and what your options are? Head over to our Cookies Policy or just check out the Cookies section right here in our Privacy Policy.
