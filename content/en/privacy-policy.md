### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to monitor activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking technologies we employ include beacons, tags, and scripts, which are used to collect and track info, and to improve and analyze our Service. The tech we use may include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is just a tiny file that lands on your Device. You can totally tell your browser to say "no thanks!" to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's trying to sneak in. But heads up, if you don't accept Cookies, some bits of our Service might not play nice. Unless you've already tweaked your browser settings to block 'em, our Service might just be using 'em!
- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These awesome files let the Company (for example) count users who have visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like recording the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish as soon as you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes outlined below:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital for bringing you all the awesome services available via the Website and letting you tap into its cool features. They're key to authenticating users and stopping any sneaky, fraudulent use of accounts. Seriously, without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just can't happen, and we only use 'em to get those services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have given a thumbs-up to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about making your life easier! They let us remember your choices when you're jamming on the Website, like keeping your login deets or language preferences in mind. The big goal here is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your faves every single time you visit. Sweet, right? 😉

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and what your options are? Just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy. It's all there!
