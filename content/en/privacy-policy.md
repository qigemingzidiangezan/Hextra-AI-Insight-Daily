### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep an eye on activity on our Service and store certain information. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts, all designed to collect and track info, and generally improve and analyze our Service. The technologies we employ might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is just a small file tucked away on your Device. You can totally tell your browser to ditch all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's trying to send. But, fair warning: if you don't accept Cookies, some bits of our Service might not work for you. Our Service usually uses Cookies unless you've tweaked your browser settings to give them the cold shoulder.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and even our emails might have these tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also hear them called clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs). These nifty little guys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email. They're also used for other cool website stats, like tracking how popular a certain section is or making sure our systems and servers are all good.

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial for giving you the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They're also super helpful for authenticating users and stopping any dodgy use of accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't be possible, and we *only* use them to deliver those services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies simply remember if you've given us the thumbs-up to use Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are pretty smart! They let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like your login info or what language you prefer. The whole point is to give you a smoother, more personalized experience so you don't have to keep punching in your preferences every single time you visit.

For more detailed info about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding them, swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
