### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our service and stash away some info. The tracking technologies we deploy include beacons, tags, and scripts, all designed to collect and track info, and generally make our service better and more insightful. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: These are those little files plopped onto your device. You can tell your browser to kick all cookies to the curb or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. Heads-up: if you don't play nice with cookies, some parts of our service might be off-limits. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject 'em, our service will likely use cookies.
- **Web Beacons**: Parts of our service and our emails might have tiny electronic files, known as web beacons (also called clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These bad boys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited certain pages or opened an email. They're also used for other cool website stats, like checking how popular a section is or verifying the integrity of systems and servers.

Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you're offline, while Session Cookies vanish into thin air as soon as you close your web browser. Poof!

We're rocking both Session and Persistent Cookies for these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies** 🔑
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These cookies are, like, super important for giving you the services available through the website and letting you use some of its cool features. They're a big help in authenticating users and preventing any sneaky, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without 'em, the services you're asking for can't happen, and we only use these cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies** ✅
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These cookies are all about checking if users have given the thumbs-up to using cookies on the website.

- **Functionality Cookies** 🧠
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the website, like stashing your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these cookies is to give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to keep punching in your preferences every single time you visit the website. Sweet!

For more deets on the cookies we use and your options, be sure to hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy. It's all there!
